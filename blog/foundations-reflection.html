What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

I've learned that I get stressed easily over the things I'm not able to do by myself.
I love learning new skills but when I get stuck, I spend hours on it to figure it out by myself.
I've realised that is a waste of my valuable learning time, so I'll try and ask questions after I've tried to solve the problem for about 30 minutes.
I also learned that it is very important to practice mindfullness. With lots of practice, I'm starting to gain more knowledge of how to concentrate on my current surroundings and how to ease my emotions.
Lastly, I learned that it's always important to focus what the other person is saying to me. From the listening and looping exercise, I could really feel the connection between me and my partner, which gave me a lot of confidence and a heart warming experience.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

The values play an important role in both learning and programming because these help us to enhance our skills and develop.
It helps everyone towards in decision making, which is essential in our everyday lives and also during our learning journey and programming.
Empathy is also important to recognise what everyone's feeling and how to interact with those emotions.
We all need to interact with various people in our lives, at school, work, home, etc. Without empathy, and understanding of what the other person is feeling/going through right now, it will be very difficult to develop a deep relationship with them.
I think that self-awareness plays the most significant role in learning and programming.
Some people may be stressed much easily compared to others, or go through a lot of roller-coaster of emotions through out the learning journey.
It's really import to be self-aware of what our own emotions are, and make sure our actions don't come from the emotions.
This means that we all need to be able to control our emotions so that when it comes to decision making, we are all able to think more obejectively; hence not deciding on something important from our emotions.
